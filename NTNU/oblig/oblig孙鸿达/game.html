Foodsaving Game

Share with the world with #GameAgainstFoodWaste

Game descrption

Played with 4 or more different groups of 3-6 people. Save the food, don't waste it! Your goal is to save the food in your home. Each group starts with 100 points and will lose 10 points each time a group member or group member's family throw edible food or losing 5 points for letting the food be spoiled. At the end of the week, tell your group how it went and how many points you have left. The group with the most remaining points left wins!
