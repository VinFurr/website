Losing 25 000 to Hunger Every Day

25 000 dies every day from starvation according to UN's website

Hunger Pandemic

The chief of WFP, who recently won the Nobel Peace Prize 2020, talked about this problem in his speech as call for action to avert Hunger Pandemic. You can read more about that at https://news.un.org.

What causes starvation?

There are many reasons that cause starvation, but one of the biggest reasons are due to climate change. Climate change in the most vunerable areas has caused drought which again caused the crops in different countries to fail according to Oxfam International. UN has earlier warned about the connection between climate change and global hunger at https://unfccc.int

There is still hope

There is still hope to turn this around. The first step is to make people aware of the problem so we would ask you to tell everyone you know about this problem. Your Friends, family and your classmates.

The second step is to stop wasting food. this is where you can help in out little "game".
