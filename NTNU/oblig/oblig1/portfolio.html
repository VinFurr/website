One of my works that i am most proud of was during the high school where i was building electrical installations. It was a lot of fun and i learned a lot about electrical & electronical systems, security systems and more!

Electrical installation

My 1st part in this topic is about general electrical installation. During high schol where i studied electro, i have learned a lot of electic installations and systems. My primarily job was to plan, draw and build the system/installation and perform a final check. I was one of the best in my class and the systems i buildt rarely needed troubleshoot as they worked perfectly most of the time. In this video, you will see one of the most complex system i built. The two light bulb in the bottom is supposed to simulate heat cables, the light bulb in the middle is being controlled by two different switches and the the left and right light bulb is controlled by one double switch. The power output in both sides is also being active all the time no matter what position the switches is set to.

Alarm system

Here is a small security system installation i built on my own. In this system, there are typical components thhat is also used in the real world such as alarm terminal, alarm central, IR detectors and magnetic contacts. Unfortunately, i did not install a the glass break detector because there were no glass to install it on. Connecting the detectors to the alarm central was surprisingly easy and i made sure that the alarm central would detect which sone the problem occured based on the different detectores connected to the different ports.
